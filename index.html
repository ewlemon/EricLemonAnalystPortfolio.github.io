Featured Project: Career Trends Dashboard (Tableau)

A multi-panel Tableau dashboard analyzing long-term U.S. employment trends using data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics.

Highlights

  • Examines occupational projections and employment characteristics across major industries
  • Identifies high-growth sectors, declining fields, and long-term workforce patterns
  • Uses interactive filters for turnover percentage, growth rate, occupation change, average annual wage, and experience and degree obtained
  • Designed as the final project for a Data Visualization course, emphasizing clarity and storytelling
  • Demonstrates ability to clean, structure, and visualize large public datasets

Project Summary

This dashboard explores nationwide employment projections and occupational trends using BLS labor statistics. The goal was to uncover insights into which fields are expanding or contracting, and to present this information in a way that is accessible to both technical and non-technical audiences. The interactive design encourages users to explore growth rates, median wages, and workforce distribution across industries. The main intended audience includes career advisors, job seekers, and HR professionals looking to understand labor market dynamics. This project showcases my ability to transform raw workforce data into meaningful, visually compelling stories that support data-driven decision-making.
